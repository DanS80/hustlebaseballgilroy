About Hustle Baseball

Welcome to Hustle Baseball, established in 2024. We are dedicated to developing young athletes both on and off the field.

Our Vision

To be the leading youth baseball organization that inspires excellence through sportsmanship, fosters personal and athletic growth, and celebrates the hustle and determination of every player. We envision a vibrant community where young athletes thrive, families connect, and the values of teamwork and respect shape future leaders both on and off the field.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a love for youth baseball by cultivating a culture of sportsmanship, relentless hustle, and personal growth. We are committed to building a strong sense of community, where players, families, and supporters come together to inspire the next generation to excel both on and off the field. Through teamwork, dedication, and respect, we strive to create an environment that champions the values of character development and lifelong passion for the love of the game.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to empower young athletes through the game of baseball, teaching the values of sportsmanship, growth, and hustle. We aim to build a supportive community that nurtures character, encourages personal development, and instills a lifelong love for the sport, creating a positive impact on players, families, and the world around them.

Our Coaches

Coach Mike

Coach Mike brings over 15 years of baseball experience to our team. His passion for developing young talent and focus on fundamental skills has helped countless players reach their full potential. Mike believes in building not just better players, but better individuals through the values of teamwork and dedication.

Coach Bill

With a background in competitive baseball and youth development, Coach Bill specializes in pitching mechanics and batting techniques. His energetic coaching style and positive approach create an encouraging environment where players can grow and excel. Bill emphasizes the importance of mental preparation alongside physical training.

About Our Team

Hustle Baseball, established in 2024, is more than just a baseball team - we're a community dedicated to developing young athletes both on and off the field. Our program focuses on:

  • Building strong fundamental baseball skills
  • Developing leadership and teamwork
  • Creating a positive and supportive environment
  • Preparing players for high school and beyond

Based in Gilroy, California, we compete in various tournaments throughout the region, giving our players exposure to different levels of competition. Our commitment to excellence and player development has made us one of the emerging forces in youth baseball.
