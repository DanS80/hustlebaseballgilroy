Tournament Sites

USSSA Tournament - Big League Dreams

Location: Manteca, CA

Venue Information:

Address: 1077 Milo Candini Drive, Manteca, CA 95337

Phone: (209) 824-2400

Website: Big League Dreams Manteca

Facility Amenities:
  • Six scaled-down replicas of Major League Baseball stadiums
  • Batting cages
  • Concession stands with food and drinks
  • Stadium Club Restaurant and Sports Bar
  • Pro shop
  • Ample parking

Notes: Please arrive 60 minutes before game time. Outside food and drinks are NOT permitted.

NCTB Tournament - Twin Creeks Sports Complex

Location: Sunnyvale, CA

Venue Information:

Address: 1100 Twin Creeks Drive, Sunnyvale, CA 94087

Phone: (408) 730-7506

Website: Twin Creeks Sports Complex

Facility Amenities:
  • Multiple baseball and softball fields
  • Synthetic turf playing surfaces
  • Stadium seating and bleachers
  • Concession stand with food and beverages
  • Restroom facilities
  • Large parking area
  • Team dugouts with bench seating

Notes: Part of the Northern California Travel Baseball (NCTB) network. Check with tournament organizers for specific field assignments and arrival times.
