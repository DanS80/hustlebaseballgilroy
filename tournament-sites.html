CCYB Tournament - Manzanita Fields

Location: Prunedale, CA

Venue Information:

Address: 10301 Seymour St, Prunedale, CA 93907

Website: Central Coast Youth Baseball (CCYB)

Facility Amenities:
  • Multiple well-maintained baseball fields
  • Concession stand with food and drinks
  • Restroom facilities
  • Ample parking
  • Family-friendly environment

Notes: Manzanita Park is home to Central Coast Youth Baseball (CCYB) tournaments. Please check with your coach for field assignments and arrival times. Outside food and drinks may be restricted during tournament events.

USSSA Tournament - Big League Dreams

Location: Manteca, CA

Venue Information:

Address: 1077 Milo Candini Drive, Manteca, CA 95337

Phone: (209) 824-2400

Website: Big League Dreams Manteca

Facility Amenities:
  • Six scaled-down replicas of Major League Baseball stadiums
  • Batting cages
  • Concession stands with food and drinks
  • Stadium Club Restaurant and Sports Bar
  • Pro shop
  • Ample parking

Notes: Please arrive 60 minutes before game time. Outside food and drinks are NOT permitted.
